# View Your Portfolio on Merak

![Portfolio UI Screenshot](/sui/portfolio.png)

## How to View Your Portfolio

This guide will help you get an overview of your assets and positions.

---

### 1. Open the Portfolio Page

Click `Portfolio` in the top menu.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. Overview

View your total assets, positions, and recent activity.

---

> **Note:** Portfolio data updates after each transaction.
