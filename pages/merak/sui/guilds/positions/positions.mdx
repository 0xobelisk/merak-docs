# View Your Positions on Merak

![Positions UI Screenshot](/sui/positions.png)

## How to View and Manage Your Positions

This guide will help you view and manage your liquidity positions.

---

### 1. Open the Positions Page

Click `Positions` in the top menu.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. View Your Positions

All your active liquidity positions will be listed here. You can manage or remove liquidity as needed.

---

> **Note:** Ensure you have enough SUI to cover transaction fees for any management actions.
