# View Your Assets on Merak

![Assets UI Screenshot](/sui/assets.png)

## How to View Your Assets

This guide will help you check your token balances.

---

### 1. Open the Assets Page

Click `Assets` in the top menu.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. View Your Balances

All your token balances will be displayed here.

---

> **Note:** If you don't see your assets, ensure your wallet is connected.
