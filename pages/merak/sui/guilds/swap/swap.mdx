# Use Merak to Swap Tokens

![Swap UI Screenshot](/sui/swap.png)

## How to Swap Tokens on Merak

This guide will walk you through swapping tokens using the Merak interface.

---

### 1. Open the Swap Page

Click `Swap` in the top menu.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. Select Tokens

Choose the token you want to swap from and the token you want to receive.

---

### 4. Enter Amount

Input the amount to swap, or click `Max`.

---

### 5. Review and Swap

Check the estimated output and click `Swap`. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

---

### 6. Check Your Balance

After the transaction, your new token balance will be updated.

---

> **Note:** Ensure you have enough SUI to cover transaction fees.
