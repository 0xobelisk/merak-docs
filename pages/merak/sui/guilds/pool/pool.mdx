# Use Merak to Add Liquidity

![Pool UI Screenshot](/sui/liquidity.png)

## How to Add Liquidity on Merak

This guide will walk you through adding liquidity to a pool.

---

### 1. Open the Pool Page

Click `Pool` in the top menu.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. Select Pool

Choose the token pair you want to provide liquidity for.

---

### 4. Enter Amounts

Input the amounts for each token.

---

### 5. Add Liquidity

Click `Add Liquidity` and confirm the transaction.

---

### 6. Check Your LP Tokens

After the transaction, you will receive LP tokens representing your share in the pool.

---

> **Note:** Ensure you have enough SUI to cover transaction fees.
