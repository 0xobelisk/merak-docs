# Use Merak to Unwrap SUI Assets

![Unwrap UI Screenshot](/sui/unwrap.png)

## How to Unwrap SUI on Merak

This guide will walk you through the process of unwrapping your wrapped SUI tokens (wSUI) back to SUI.

---

### 1. Open the Unwrap Page

Navigate to the top menu and click on `Unwrap` to enter the unwrapping interface.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Click the wallet button in the top right corner to connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. Select Wrapped Token

In the `Source Token` dropdown, select the wrapped token (e.g., wSUI) you want to unwrap.

---

### 4. Enter Unwrap Amount

Enter the amount of wSUI you wish to unwrap, or click `Max` to use your full available balance.

---

### 5. Confirm and Unwrap

Click the `Unwrap` button and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

---

### 6. Check Your SUI Balance

After the transaction, your SUI balance will be updated.

---

> **Note:** Ensure you have enough SUI to cover transaction fees.
