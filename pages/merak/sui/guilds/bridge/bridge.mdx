# Use Merak to Bridge Assets

![Bridge UI Screenshot](/sui/bridge.png)

## How to Bridge Assets on Merak

This guide will walk you through bridging assets between networks.

---

### 1. Open the Bridge Page

Click `Bridge` in the top menu.

---

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Connect your SUI wallet.

---

### 3. Select Source and Destination

Choose the source and destination networks and tokens.

---

### 4. Enter Amount

Input the amount to bridge.

---

### 5. Confirm and Bridge

Click `Bridge` and confirm the transaction.

---

### 6. Check Your Assets

After bridging, check your assets on the destination network.

---

> **Note:** Bridging may take a few minutes to complete.
